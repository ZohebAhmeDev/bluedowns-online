Zoheb Ahmed, an internet entrepreneur from Cape Town, established Blue Downs Online™.

Blue Downs Online™ was first established as a comprehensive communication tool for residents, businesses, and organizations within the Blue Downs municipal area. Since then, this website has grown exponentially and become a prominent, one-of-a-kind portal for the Cape Town/Western Cape region, with some of its portals now serving a national audience. Thanks to its thousands of Top 10 rankings on www.google.com, this website has become a critical resource for businesses looking for extensive, high-quality online exposure.

Blue Downs Online™ aims to:

  • Foster effective communication within the Cape Town and South African community through a powerful online tool.
  • Offer innovative and efficient marketing channels to businesses and organizations within the Cape Town and South African community.
  • To establish Blue Downs Online™ as a well-known household brand within the Cape Town and South African community.
  • To operate Blue Downs Online™ in accordance with sound business principles, both internally and externally, in order to ensure its success.
